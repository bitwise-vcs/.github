## About Us

Bitwise enabling innovation with software engineering excellence. We are a design services company specialising in the development of embedded and application software. Established for over 30 years, we have long-term relationships with some of the world’s leading technology companies. We provide software and hardware consultancy, design, development, test and system integration services. Operating in multiple sectors including medical, aerospace, energy & industrial, transportation & automotive, communications and financial services, many of our clients share similar requirements for real-time, safety-critical operation, high reliability and outstanding quality control. Our solutions can be found in products ranging from simple card payment terminals to complex radiotherapy and secure systems. Bitwise is ISO9001/27001/13485 accredited and we have operations in the Scotland, Sweden and the Netherlands. Our headquarters are in Dunfermline, Scotland. Bitwise services solve a number of business challenges including: • Accelerating execution of the product road-map • Developing strategies for new technology or architectures • Increasing R&D flexibility to handle development peaks with confidence • Scoping and capturing product requirements • Prolonging product life by adding new features • Improving the performance and maintainability of systems • Providing independent validation and verification • Widening test coverage and increasing throughput via automation
